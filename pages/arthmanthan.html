Arthmanthan

Arthmanthan is SPADE's annual research journal. The objective is to give the young graduates an insight into the field of research and to encourage them to explore their own potential as successful writers.

In order to prepare its members for writing a research paper or article, SPADE organizes a small workshop before the work on Arthmanthan begins. This workshop gives members new experiences and benefits in the field of research.
